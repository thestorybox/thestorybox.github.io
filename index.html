The Official Story Box

This blog is meant to be a repository for all my stories and writing guides.
Thanks for stopping by!

“Reading is to the mind what exercise is to the body.”
― Joseph Addison

I loved writing stories as a kid. I would get a bright idea, and then I would scribble it down on my notebook for safekeeping. But sometimes, the notebooks would get thrown away, or damaged, or lost. Other times, I would erase the stories myself. In the end, only two or three stories managed to survive.


A few years later, I will get to read these again. I would look back at how I thought and wrote in the past. I'll find how much I've grown as a writer. And then I'll make fun of myself for making a stupid story/grammatical errors/assumptions that anyone actually would read this.

I look forward to doing that.
